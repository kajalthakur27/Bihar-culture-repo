Welcome to Bihar

Bihar is one of the oldest inhabited regions of the world, known for its rich cultural heritage, ancient universities like Nalanda, and spiritual landmarks like Bodh Gaya. It is the land of Lord Buddha, great thinkers like Chanakya, and powerful empires like the Mauryas and Guptas. Bihar is also famous for its folk art, Madhubani paintings, classical music, festivals like Chhath, and delicious food like litti-chokha. This land reflects simplicity, knowledge, and unity in diversity.
